Abstract

This project aims to build an AI-driven job recommendation system that revolutionizes how job seekers find employment and how employers connect with top talent. By leveraging Vultrâ€™s cloud services and advanced machine learning techniques, the system will analyze user profiles, job descriptions, and behavioral patterns to offer personalized job recommendations in real-time.

With features such as skill-gap analysis, AI-driven job matching, and real-time notifications, the platform will streamline job search processes and improve the overall user experience.

Problem Statement

Existing job portals lack personalization and effective job matching, often overwhelming users with irrelevant job postings. The process of connecting job seekers with appropriate opportunities is time-consuming, and many platforms fail to provide targeted recommendations that align with user profiles and preferences.

Proposed Solution

The AI-Based Job Recommendation System will solve this issue by implementing an AI-driven recommendation engine that matches users to jobs based on their profiles, resumes, and activity on the platform.

  • Hybrid Recommendation System: Combining collaborative and content-based filtering.
  • Skill-Gap Analysis: Recommending relevant skills to enhance employability.

Use of Vultr Services

  • Compute Instances: Running machine learning models and real-time recommendation services.
  • Managed Databases: Storing user profiles, job data, and interaction logs.
  • Object Storage: Managing resumes, job descriptions, and user-uploaded data.
  • Block Storage: Backup and data persistence for secure information management.
  • Vultr Kubernetes Engine: Scaling the platform to accommodate a growing user base.
  • Load Balancers: Ensuring high availability and performance for the recommendation system.

Target Audience

  • Job Seekers: Individuals actively looking for job opportunities.
  • Employers/Recruiters: Companies searching for candidates who meet specific job criteria.
  • Educational Institutions: Helping students and graduates identify career opportunities.

Expected Outcomes

  • Successfully matching job seekers with suitable job opportunities.
  • Improving job search efficiency and user satisfaction.
  • Assisting employers in screening candidates with AI-powered recommendations.
  • Establishing a scalable, cloud-based platform capable of supporting a large user base.
